Full text: Â  Test Positive Aware Network (TPAN) received a grant for general operations in the amount of $115,000 from the Alphawood Foundation.
For more than 27 years, TPAN has delivered HIV/AIDS-related programs for hundreds of individuals each year who are Living below the federal poverty Level and impacted by HIV/AIDS.
In addition to Alphawood, TPAN was recently awarded a $20,000 grant from the AIDS Foundation of Chicago through its Challenge Grant program.
TPAN will Launch a new prevention program with the funding, "Lifeline," that will provide an evidence-based intervention for people who test negative but are still vulnerable to HIV.
Alphawood awards grants to more than 200 organizations annually, primarily in the areas of advocacy, architecture and preservation, arts, domestic violence prevention, the environment, promotion and protection of the rights of LGBT ritizens and people living with HIV/AIDS, and other human and civil rights.
