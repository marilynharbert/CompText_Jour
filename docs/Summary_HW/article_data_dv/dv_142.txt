The members honored Cook County Board President Toni Preckwinkle and Teatro Luna Theater Group.
Preckwinkle has been active in supporting women issues and family issues in her more than 20 years of public service as an elected official. Teatro Luna is the only pan-Latina theatre troupe in the country.
CNOW focuses on reproductive rights, women's health, ending violence against women, economic equality and Lesbian rights.
